Learning Features

Interactive Flashcards

Test yourself on word meanings. Type your answer and get instant feedback. Words you find difficult will reappear for review.

Spaced Repetition

Words are scheduled for review based on how well you know them. Easy words appear less frequently, hard words appear more often.

Audio Pronunciation

Click the play button on any word card to hear the correct pronunciation. This helps with listening and speaking skills.

Progress Tracking

Track your mastery from HSK 1 to HSK 6. See which words you've mastered and which need more practice.

Learning Tips

Daily Practice

Try to learn at least 5-10 new words every day. Consistency is key to language learning.

Listen and Repeat

Always use the audio feature. Listen carefully to the pronunciation and try to repeat it out loud.

Visual Learning

Pay attention to the character shapes. Chinese is a visual language, so recognizing patterns helps with memorization.
